Join Us

Interested in becoming a member of the Hacking and Cyber Security Club? Here's how you can get involved:
  1. Weekly Meetings: Join us every Thursday at 6:00PM in Coover 1011 for our weekly meetings. No prior experience is necessary, just show up and participate! Check our events calendar for the latest schedule.
  2. Discord Community: Connect with us on Discord, where our community stays active and engaged between meetings. Join discussions, share resources, and connect with fellow cybersecurity enthusiasts. Join here
  3. Contact Us: Have questions or need more information? Feel free to reach out to us at hacc-cabinet@iastate.edu, or the cabinet in person if you have any questions or suggestions!
